About

EthCC (Ethereum Community Conference) is a conference that will take place in Paris at the Conservatoire Nationale des Arts et Métiers (CNAM) on the 8th to 10th March 2018. It features:

  • 4 amphitheaters
  • Multiple streams : Scalability, Anonymity, Development tools, Governance Compliance...
  • Small workshops for developers & researchers
  • Networking and experience sharing
